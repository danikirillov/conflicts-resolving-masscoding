## Creating an Account

### Registration

1. Click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner
2. Fill in your details:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
3. Accept the terms and conditions
4. Click "Create Account"

You'll receive a welcome email with a special discount code!

### Logging In

1. Click "Log In" in the navigation bar
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Sign In"

## Browsing Products

### Viewing All Products

Navigate to the Products page to see our full catalog of available pets.

### Filtering Options

Use the sidebar filters to narrow down your search:

- **Category**: Dogs, Cats, Birds, Fish, Reptiles
- **Price Range**: Adjust the slider to set your budget
- **Age**: Young, Adult, Senior
- **Size**: Small, Medium, Large

### Sorting Products

Sort products by:
- Name (A-Z)
- Price (Low to High / High to Low)
- Newest Arrivals
- Most Popular

### Product Details

Click on any product to view detailed information:
- High-quality images
- Breed information
- Age and size
- Health records
- Personality traits
- Care requirements

## Managing Your Cart

### Adding Items

Click the "Add to Cart" button on any product page.

### Viewing Your Cart

Click the cart icon in the top right corner to review your items.

### Updating Quantities

- Use the + and - buttons to adjust quantities
- Click "Remove" to delete an item from your cart

### Applying Promo Codes

Enter your promo code in the designated field and click "Apply".

## Placing Orders

### Checkout Process

1. **Review Your Cart**: Ensure all items are correct
2. **Shipping Information**:
   - Enter your delivery address
   - Provide contact information
3. **Payment Method**:
   - Credit/Debit Card
   - PayPal
   - Apple Pay
4. **Review & Confirm**: Double-check all details
5. **Place Order**: Complete your purchase

### Order Confirmation

You'll receive:
- An immediate on-screen confirmation
- A confirmation email with order details
- Tracking information once shipped

### Tracking Your Order

Check your order status:
1. Log in to your account
2. Go to "My Orders"
3. Click on the order number to see tracking details
